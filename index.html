Why independent matters

An unbiased authority on your power system — not a sales channel for one.

EASTENGHOUSE functions as an unbiased engineering and testing authority, fully independent of the manufacturers, suppliers, and installers of the equipment we assess. When a vendor tests their own gear, the incentive is to pass it. When we test it, the incentive is to tell you the truth.

That independence runs through every study, every commissioning report, and every maintenance recommendation we deliver — so decisions about your power system are based on data, not a sales relationship.
